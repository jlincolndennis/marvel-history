About

Marvel comics has opened up a series of APIs to their complete publishing history database. Providing this data to comics fans is very exciting, but with so much data available, it can be hard to find interesting information if you are not looking for something specific.

This Week In Marvel History is a web app that will show you notable Marvel titles released historically corresponding to a specific date.

Help

Use the dropdown list below to select a date and view Marvel titles released on that week throught history.

Titles are sorted by "Age" as follows:

  • Modern Age: 1986 - Present
  • Bronze Age: 1970 - 1985
  • Silver Age: 1956 - 1970
  • Golden Age: 1939 - 1955
